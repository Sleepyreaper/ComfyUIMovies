# EAGLE FORCE — Theme / Trailer Music (ElevenLabs, instrumental)

Toggle **instrumental only**. 60s (trim to ~35s at mux, or I fade it).

**Prompt:**
An epic, high-energy 1980s Saturday-morning cartoon action theme, fully
instrumental, about 60 seconds, heroic and triumphant in a bright major key.
Big brassy synth-brass fanfare, driving eighth-note analog synth bass, punchy
gated reverb drums with loud snare and toms, soaring lead synth melody, and
heroic orchestral horns layered on top — the sound of G.I. Joe, M.A.S.K., and
Transformers title themes. Structure it like a trailer: (0-6s) a dark, ominous
low synth-brass swell with a menacing minor motif for the villain; (6-10s) a
sharp drum fill that BREAKS into the heroic main theme; (10-30s) the full
driving anthem, fast and pumping with a catchy triumphant hook that repeats and
gets stuck in your head; (30-40s) a huge climactic key-change and a final
pounding brass-and-drum HIT for the logo reveal, then a quick punchy button.
Bold, cheesy, nostalgic, adrenaline, heroic, retro analog synths. No vocals.
